Model Construction

Problem Restatement

Our task is to predict a student's Class according to its 16 features. This can be interrepted as two kinds of problems.

  • Classification

Treat Class_1, Class_2 and Class_3 as three categories. Comparison between these categories is meaningless.

  • Regression

Class_1, Class_2, Class_3 are assumed ordered with fixed distance. For example, the best students may be in Class_3, middle students in Class_2 and poorest students in Class_1. In addition, distance between Class_1 and Class_2, Class_2 and Class_3 are the same.

In our task, Class_1, Class_2, and Class_3 are indeed ordered, but the distance between two neighboring classes may not be the same. Therefore, we tried the above two ways to solve the problem.

Model Selection

For classification models, there are SVM classifier, logistic classifier, random forest classifier. For regression models, there are linear regresser, random forest regresser.

There are 10 category features in our task, 6 of them are binary features, which means it's not suitable to apply SVM, logistic or linear model. Therefore, we choose random forest classifier and random forest regresser.

Introduction to Random Forest
Principle

Random forest is composed of many decision trees. Every decision tree voted for a result. Random forest choose result with the most votes as final result.

The principle of decision tree is a series of if-else. Prediction starts at root node, and destination node of every downward step is determined by feature of the current node. When reaching a leaf node, a prediction result is yielded.

Advantage
  • Expecially good at processing category feature, whose values are discrete and may be without order.
  • Highly parallelized, since the training of trees can run in parallel.
  • Anti overfitting, since the final result is based on different trees.
Disadvantage
  • Poor at processing data with low dimensional feature.
  • Work like a black box. It's hard to control operation inside random forest.
  • Features with large value range have greater influence on random forest.
